Billy Woman

Billycom is the brainchild of Petra Meeus. With more than 20 years of experience in PR, Marketing & Communication with, among others, Concentra Media, RTV ROB-tv, British American Tobacco and CarsOnTheWeb, she decided to set up her own label so that she can work more independently and in a more project-based manner. In the meantime she has rolled out various projects for several clients in the IT sector (DataFlow and ONE Agency), the industrial sector (Sumitomo Genk and Vyncolit Ghent) and the artistic sector (Le Concert Olympique, a classic Beethoven orchestra, and AtelierP, an artist).
